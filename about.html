Our story?

#Yours.

Imagine arriving at your grand hotel in Paris. You drop your bags, take in the view of the Eiffel Tower, and call the concierge for a taxi, dinner reservation, or perhaps a special request. But what if you need more? That's when we take the leap with serving your life with #MOREFRANCE.



Our name?

*Croa2ss-ant1

*A playful nod to les rosbifs calling us frogs—so we croak in English while enjoying our favorite pastry: croissant.

1 In French, verbs ending in -ant show an ongoing action, just like -ing in English.

2 And while our fellow French might correct us, saying, 'It’s coassant for a frog croaking in French, not croassant,' that’s exactly the point—we mix English and French like no one else, just like we do with our services, always ready to help you if French gives you a frog in your throat.
